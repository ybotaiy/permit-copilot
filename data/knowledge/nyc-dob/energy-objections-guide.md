# DOB NOW: Build – Energy Objections & Appointments

This guide covers how Delegates associated with a DOB NOW: Build Job Filing (including Energy Submission) can resolve Energy Plan Examiner Objections and schedule meetings through the DOB NOW portal.

## Overview

- Delegates per their role in the DOB NOW: Build filing process will be alerted in the event that an Energy Plan Examiner (PE) makes/adds an objection(s) during their review.
- If the Energy Plan Examiner examining your Energy Submission makes an objection, you will receive an email inviting you to log into DOB NOW to resolve the issue and to schedule a meeting, if the appointment is allowed.

## System Guidelines

- Fields with a red asterisk (*) are required and must be completed.
- Grayed-out fields are Read-Only or are auto-populated by the system.
- Depending on the size of your screen or browser you may have to use the scroll bar to view more options or full fields.

## Resolving Objection(s)

### Step 1: Navigate to Energy Submission Dashboard

Click the Dashboard Menu to select the **Energy Submission** Dashboard within the Requests section.

### Step 2: Locate the Objection

The Energy Submission Dashboard displays. The Energy Submission with the objection(s) displays a Request Status of **"Objections"**. Click the Energy Submission to view the details.

### Step 3: Confirm Request Information

A Request Information confirmation pop-up displays showing the Request Number, Associated Job Filing Number, and Address. Click **OK** to proceed.

### Step 4: View the Objections/Appointments Tab

The system displays a red exclamation mark (!) within the status bar and a new **Objections/Appointments** tab. Click the tab to view the objections.

### Step 5: View Objection Details

To view the details of the Objection, click the drop-down arrow or click the **Edit** icon.

### Step 6: Review Objection Information

The Objection details display with:

- **Objection** — the issue raised by the Energy Plan Examiner
- **Objection Detail** — specific information about the objection
- **Code** — reference to Building Codes (e.g., RCNY)
- **Work Type** — e.g., Mechanical Systems
- **Code Type** — e.g., RCNY-14SG
- **Created Date** — when the objection was made
- **Created By** — the Plan Examiner who made the objection
- **Objection Status** — Open or Resolved (required field)
- **Comments** — space to respond to the Energy Plan Examiner (required field)
- **Objection History** — record of all status changes and comments

### Step 7: Update Objection Status

Select an **Objection Status** from the dropdown:
- **Open** — objection remains unresolved
- **Resolved** — objection has been addressed

### Step 8: Enter Comments

Enter **Comments** providing your response to the Plan Examiner (255 characters maximum).

### Step 9: Save Changes

Click **Update** to save your changes.

### Step 10: Re-Attest

The Applicant of Record is required to re-attest before Resubmitting. Click the **Statements & Signatures** tab and click the checkbox. The Name and Date fields are auto-populated.

### Step 11: Resubmit

If an appointment is not allowed or you choose not to schedule an appointment, click **Save** and then **Resubmit** to complete resolving the objection.

## Scheduling an Appointment

**Note:** If the Energy Plan Examiner did not indicate an Appointment is allowed, the Appointment button will be disabled.

### Step 1: Add Appointment

Directly below the Objections box is the DOB Appointments window. Click the **+ Appointment** button to begin the scheduling process.

### Step 2: Complete Appointment Fields

The New Appointment pop-up window displays with the following required fields:

- **Required Attendees** — add the applicable attendee(s)
- **Select Appointment Date** — choose from the calendar (holidays and fully booked dates are indicated)
- **Select Appointment Time** — choose from available time slots
- **Work Types** — select applicable work types (e.g., General Construction)
- **Meeting Intent** — describe why you would like to meet to resolve this objection (255 characters maximum)

The Plan Examiner name and Job Filing Number are auto-populated.

### Step 3: Schedule

Click **Schedule** to save the information entered.

### Step 4: Confirm

The system displays a **Confirm** pop-up window with the details of the appointment (date and time). Click **Yes** to proceed.

### Step 5: Confirmation

A notification pop-up window confirms the Appointment has been scheduled. Click **OK** to close.

### Step 6: Re-Attest After Appointment

After your appointment and/or you've resolved the Objection, the Applicant of Record is required to re-attest via the **Statements & Signatures** tab.

### Step 7: Save and Resubmit

Click **Save** and then **Resubmit** to complete resolving the objection.

## Canceling an Appointment

### Step 1: Find the Appointment

From within the Appointment Grid, find the applicable appointment and click **Cancel**.

### Step 2: Confirm Cancellation

The Confirm pop-up window displays. Click **Yes** to proceed.

### Step 3: Verify Cancellation

The system displays the Appointment Status as **Cancelled** within the appointment grid.
