# Second Review of Plan Examination Objections

## Determination Fees

Per the Rules of the Department 1 RCNY 101-03, Determination Requests submitted to the NYC Department of Buildings for review are subject to the following fees:

1. **CCD1 or ZRD1 request for Determination** — $1,000
   - Pre-Determination (pre-filed job) Request
   - Request for variation of a Code requirement or MDL section 277.16
   - Appeal of an affirmation of an objection after a second plan examination review

2. **Appeal of CCD1 or ZRD1 Determination** — $2,500

**Exemptions:** Properties that are exempt from fees per §28-112.1 and 1 RCNY 101-03.

## Second Review Requirement

A determination request for a plan examination objection must go through a **second plan examination request** first. These second review requests are covered by the filing fee and do not require additional fees.

## Requesting a Review of Plan Examination Objections (AI-1 Form)

The applicant must submit an **AI-1 form** listing the objection and providing reasons for its removal.

Only rejections, following a second review, may be submitted as a Determination request and are subject to determination fees.

Requests to review plan examination objections must be submitted on a completed AI-1 form with all supporting documentation in **one flattened PDF format** to the appropriate email address listed below.

## Review of Plan Examination Objections

The appropriate borough office will review all requests for second review of plan examination objection(s). Requests will either be resolved, rejected, or the applicant will have to meet with the Department for further review.

To review with an applicant, the borough office will schedule an appointment and the applicant must be present during the appointment to review the objection. All relevant information, including disapproved plans, must be made available for review.

## After Rejection

When a borough office rejects a second review request of a plan examination objection, the applicant may revise the plans or submit a determination request to appeal the affirmation of an objection.

A separate determination request must be submitted by the applicant.

## Requesting a Determination

- To request a **Zoning Determination**, file a **Zoning Resolution Determination Form (ZRD1)**
- To request a **Construction Code Determination**, file a **Construction Code Determination Form (CCD1)**

## Determination Submission Requirements

- Include a **copy of the invoice** from the Borough Office where the fee was paid, except for properties exempt under §28-112.1 and the rule.
- Forms must be **signed and sealed by a professional**.
- Determination requests are limited to **one per form** and must cite all related Code or zoning sections.
- Any attachments must be formatted on **11" x 17" paper or smaller**.
