# Second Review of Plan Examination Objections

## Determination Fees

Effective January 28, 2018, per Rules of the Department 1 RCNY 101-03, Determination requests submitted to the Department for review are subject to the following fees:

1. **CCD1 or ZRD1 request for Determination** — $1,000
   - Pre-Determination (pre-filed job) Request
   - Request for variation of a Code requirement or MDL section 277.16
   - Appeal of an affirmation of an objection after a second plan examination review

2. **Appeal of CCD1 or ZRD1 Determination** — $2,500

**Exemptions:** Properties that are exempt from fees per New York City Administrative Code (AC) 28-112.1 and 1 RCNY 101-03.

## Second Plan Examination Review Requirement

Before a Determination request for plan examination objection is submitted, a **second plan examination review** is required. The second review request is covered by the filing fee and does not require additional fees.

## Requesting a Second Review (AI-1 Form)

An applicant requests a second review of plan examination objections by submitting an **AI-1 form** listing the objection and providing reasons for its removal.

Submit a completed AI-1 form, with all supporting documentation in **one flattened PDF format**, to the appropriate email address listed below.

Only rejections, following a second review, may be submitted as a Determination request and are subject to determination fees.

## Review of Plan Examination Objections

The appropriate borough office will review all requests for second review of plan examination objection(s). Requests will either be:

- **Resolved** — the objection is removed
- **Rejected** — the objection stands
- **Meeting required** — the applicant will have to meet with the Department for further review

To review with an applicant, the borough office will schedule an appointment and the **applicant must be present** during the appointment to review the objection. All relevant information, including disapproved plans, **must** be made available for review.

## After Rejection

When a borough office rejects a second review request of a plan examination objection, the applicant may:

1. **Revise the plans**, or
2. **Submit a determination request** to appeal the affirmation of an objection

A separate determination request **must** be submitted by the applicant.

## Requesting a Determination

- To request a **Zoning Determination**, file a **ZRD1: Zoning Resolution Determination Form** (Instructions available from DOB)
- To request a **Construction Code Determination**, file a **CCD1: Construction Code Determination Form** (Instructions available from DOB)

### Determination Submission Requirements

- All submissions must include a **copy of the invoice** from the Borough Office where the fee was paid, except properties that are exempt from fees per (AC) 28-112.1 and 1 RCNY 101-03.
- Forms must be **signed and sealed by a professional**.
- Determination requests are limited to **one per form** and must cite all related Code or Zoning sections.
- Any attachments (drawings or other documents) must be formatted on **11" x 17" paper or smaller**.
