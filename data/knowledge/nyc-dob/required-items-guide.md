# Required Items Reference Guide

The Required Items Reference Guide is a publication made available by the NYC Department of Buildings as a courtesy to the public. The Guide establishes a clear, common standard for when requirements apply to a particular job filing and how to satisfy each requirement.

**Note:** This version does not include Controlled Inspection Required Items or Builders Pavement Plan (BPP) Required Items (with one exception). The Guide may not be a complete list of every item required for a job.

## What is a Required Item?

Required Items indicate what requirements apply to a specific job, and when they must be satisfied. Every job filing is unique. Scope of work, location, and other variables determine which requirements apply.

## How Required Items Are Associated with a Job

A Required Item is associated with a job through:

1. **Programming in BIS** — BIS automatically adds certain requirements based upon programming logic following pre-established business rules. For example, all NB and Alt 1 jobs require Final Construction Signoff.
2. **A Plan Examiner adds the item** — During plan exam, the Plan Examiner determines which requirements apply.
3. **A filing professional selects the item** — On professionally certified jobs, the professional indicates all items that are required on the PC-1 form (checklist for Professional Certification).

## Reviewing Required Items on BISWeb

To check Required Items for a job:

1. Visit http://www.nyc.gov/buildings
2. Input the property address into the BIS web query, or navigate to BISWeb and enter the job number
3. On the Property Profile Overview, click Jobs/Filings
4. Click the job number to reach the "Application Data" screen
5. Click "Items Required" to see the required items list

### Key to the Required Items Screen

- **WHO RECV**: T = Plan Examiner Only, N = Clerical Staff
- **PRI TO**: APP = Approval, PER = Permit, SGN = Signoff
- **Required ID/Date**: Clerk who entered the item and when
- **Received ID/Date**: Clerk or plan examiner who received the item and when
- **Date Certified**: When TR-1 certification was received
- **Waiver**: "X" means the item is waived for this job

## General Required Items (Alphabetical)

### §1 Address: New House Number Approved
- **Required Prior To:** Application Approval
- **References:** GCL §35 & 36, Memo 4/1/83
- **Applicability:** Select if the application results in a new house number.
- **How to Satisfy:** Obtain house number verification stamp on back of PW-1 from Topographic Unit of Borough President's Office.

### §2 Adult Activity Approval
- **Required Prior To:** Application Approval
- **References:** ZR §32-01, §42-01; OPPN 7/02, 6/98, 5/98, 8/96, 7/96
- **Applicability:** Select when filing a professionally certified application that includes adult activity on the premises.
- **How to Satisfy:** Must submit plans for plan exam approval.

### §3 Adult Establishment Approval
- **Required Prior To:** Application Approval
- **References:** ZR §32-01, §42-01; OPPN 7/02, 6/98, 5/98, 8/96, 7/96
- **Applicability:** Select when establishing a new or enlarging an existing "adult establishment" use as defined by ZR §12-10.
- **How to Satisfy:** Submit zoning plot diagram showing conformance with ZR.

### §4 Anti-Harassment Area Checklist
- **Required Prior To:** Application Approval
- **References:** ZR §93-90, §98-70, §121-50, §23-013
- **Applicability:** Automatically applies for NB, A1, A2 (PL, OT/GC, OT-Other), A3 (OT/GC, OT-Other), subdivision, applications in Special Hudson Yards District, Special West Chelsea District, Special Garment Center P-2 Preservation Area, or Greenpoint-Williamsburg Anti-Harassment Area.
- **How to Satisfy:** Submit Anti-Harassment Area Checklist (HPD-1). If not exempt, submit HPD Certificate of No Harassment or HPD Certification of Cure Compliance.

### §5 Applications: Signoff Open Jobs
- **Required Prior To:** Signoff
- **References:** BC §26-222, MDL §301.1, NYC Charter 645(b)(3)d
- **Applicability:** Automatically applies to NB and Alt 1 applications.
- **How to Satisfy:** Ensure that all open applications have been signed off.

### §6 Asbestos ACP 7 or 5 or ASB 4
- **Required Prior To:** Application Approval
- **References:** LL 76/85, BC §27-198.1, AC §24-146.1, various TPPNs and OPPNs
- **Applicability:** Select for all Alt 1, Alt 2 and Alt 3 jobs, except Alt 1 jobs for legalization of demolition work completed prior to April 1, 1987.
- **How to Satisfy:** Submit ACP 7 (friable asbestos project), ACP 5 (friable non-asbestos project), or ASB 4 (exemption from asbestos certification).

### §7 Backflow Preventer (RPZ or Double-Check Valve Assembly)
- **Required Prior To:** Permit
- **References:** RS 16 §107.18(5), LL 67/77, OPPN 14/90, NYS Sanitary Code 5-1.31
- **Applicability:** Select for all NB, Alt 1, Alt 2 applications and ARAs where proposed work involves a new connection to public water supply or installation of a new sprinkler system.
- **How to Satisfy:** Have device tested and obtain Gen. 215B form (RPZ test letter). Submit to Plumbing Division.

### §8 Boiler (BL) Signoff
- **Required Prior To:** Signoff
- **References:** BC §27-793, OPPN 6/93, LL 62/91
- **Applicability:** Select if NB or Alt 1 has a boiler (BL) application filed under a separate job number.
- **How to Satisfy:** File PW-1C (Schedule C). Obtain BL signoff from Boiler division.

### §11 BSA Variance
- **Required Prior To:** Application Approval
- **References:** ZR §72-21, §32-31, §32-32; NYC Charter 666.6 & 666.7; MDL §310
- **Applicability:** Select for any application where the proposed work would be prohibited by Zoning Res., Admin. Code or MDL.
- **How to Satisfy:** Obtain variance from BSA. Indicate calendar number and conditions on Schedule A.

### §12 BSA/CPC Approval
- **Required Prior To:** Application Approval
- **References:** ZR §72-21, §32-31, §32-32; OPPN 16/87
- **Applicability:** Select where proposed work requires a special permit, authorization, or modification by BSA or CPC.
- **How to Satisfy:** Obtain approvals from BSA and/or CPC.

### §13 Certificate of Occupancy
- **Required Prior To:** Signoff
- **References:** BC §27-213, 217; OPPN 6/93
- **Applicability:** Automatically applies to NB and Alt 1 applications.
- **How to Satisfy:** Submit CO Application (PW-6) to CO Unit.

### §17 Cost Affidavit: Estimated
- **Required Prior To:** Application Approval
- **Applicability:** Select for Alt 1, Alt 2 and Alt 3 applications.
- **How to Satisfy:** Contractor submits cost affidavit form (PW-3) indicating estimated cost of construction.

### §18 Cost Affidavit: Actual
- **Required Prior To:** Signoff
- **Applicability:** Select for Alt 1 applications only.
- **How to Satisfy:** Submit original, typed, notarized final owner's cost affidavit form (PW-3) to fee estimator.

### §29 ECCCNYS Compliance Statement
- **Required Prior To:** Application Approval
- **References:** Energy Conservation Construction Code of New York State
- **Applicability:** Automatically applies to all New Building (NB) applications.
- **How to Satisfy:** Place appropriate statement of compliance or reason for exemption on plans.

### §32 Environmental "E" - DEP Notice to Proceed
- **Required Prior To:** Application Approval
- **References:** ZR §11-15, §93-051, §98-051, §104-05, §115-03; OPPN 2/05
- **Applicability:** Select for NB, Alt 1, Alt 2 and Alt 3 applications where work is on a lot designated with an "E" on the Zoning Map.
- **How to Satisfy:** Submit DEP Notice to Proceed, DEP Notice of No Objection, or Statement on AI-1 form.

### §40 Final Construction Signoff
- **Required Prior To:** Signoff
- **References:** BC §27-210
- **Applicability:** Automatically applies to NB and Alt 1 applications.
- **How to Satisfy:** Schedule Construction inspection and obtain signoff.

### §45 Fire Alarm (FA): FDNY Approval
- **Required Prior To:** Application Approval
- **References:** OPPN 3/91, TPPN 2/89, 1 RCNY §28-02(e)(2)
- **Applicability:** Select if the application is a fire alarm work type (except Dir. 14/75 for mini class E, cabaret, or CO detector systems).
- **How to Satisfy:** Obtain letter of approval from FDNY Bureau of Fire Prevention.

### §47 Fire Protection Plan (FPP): FDNY Approval
- **Required Prior To:** Signoff
- **References:** Dir. 1/81, BC §27-228.1 and §27-228.2
- **Applicability:** For buildings listed in BC §27-228.1, select for all NB and any alteration involving change in use, exceeding $1 million in cost, or resulting in amendments to a previously issued FPP.
- **How to Satisfy:** Submit Fire Protection Plan to FDNY and DOB.

### §50 Flood Zone: FEMA Elevation Certificate
- **Required Prior To:** Signoff
- **References:** BC §27-316(g), TPPN 1/04
- **Applicability:** Select for all NB and any alterations that are a substantial improvement in a flood zone.
- **How to Satisfy:** Have P.E./R.A. certify elevation using a FEMA elevation certificate.

### §62 Insurance: Disability
- **Required Prior To:** Permit
- **References:** BC §27-160
- **Applicability:** Automatically applies to all applications except subdivisions.
- **How to Satisfy:** Submit DB 120.1 form or waiver form WC/DB-100.

### §63 Insurance: Liability
- **Required Prior To:** Permit
- **References:** BC §27-204(b), §27-160; NYC Charter 2-20; OPPN 6/92
- **Applicability:** Automatically applies to all applications except subdivisions.
- **How to Satisfy:** Submit ACORD form from a licensed insurance broker.

### §64 Insurance: Workers' Compensation
- **Required Prior To:** Permit
- **References:** BC §27-160
- **Applicability:** Automatically applies to all applications except subdivisions.
- **How to Satisfy:** Submit DB 120.1 form and C106.2, GSA 105.2, SI 12, or U26.3 form.

### §66 Landmarks Approval
- **Required Prior To:** Application Approval
- **References:** AC §25-301-321, 1 RCNY §21-01(d), various memos and OPPNs
- **Applicability:** Select for all applications where the property is landmarked or in a landmarked district.
- **How to Satisfy:** Submit DOB drawings approved by LPC and either a "Permit: Certificate of No Effect" or "Permit: Certificate of Appropriateness."

### §81 Restriction: Restrictive Declaration
- **Required Prior To:** Application Approval
- **References:** LPPN 1/05 & 1/04, TPPN 10/87
- **Applicability:** Select if a restrictive declaration is required to comply with Code or ZR (single parcel, one party).
- **How to Satisfy:** Submit proof of recordation. For PC applications, professional must certify the declaration satisfies statutory requirements.

### §109 Violations: Clear All
- **Required Prior To:** Signoff
- **References:** NYC Charter 645(b)(3)d, AC §26-222, MDL §301.1
- **Applicability:** Automatically applies to NB and Alt 1 applications.
- **How to Satisfy:** Ensure that all open violations on the property have been cleared.

### §112–116 Zoning Exhibits I–V
- **Required Prior To:** Application Approval
- **References:** Memo 5/18/78, ZR §12-10
- **Applicability:** Select based on property ownership (single vs. multiple) and job type (NB, Alt 1, Alt 2 enlargements).
- **How to Satisfy:** Submit certified exhibits to DOB and file with County Clerk's office.

## Post-Approval Required Items

These items may be added after the initial list has been completed, typically when seeking a Temporary Certificate of Occupancy (TCO) or when a previously satisfied item has expired.

### §117 Escrow Letter (Attorney)
- **Source:** OPPN 5/92
- **Applicability:** NB or Alt 1 seeking a temporary CO for a 1, 2 or 3-family home.
- **How to Satisfy:** Submit completed Escrow Agent/Attorney letter.

### §118 Escrow Letter (Builder)
- **Source:** OPPN 5/92
- **Applicability:** NB or Alt 1 seeking a temporary CO.
- **How to Satisfy:** Submit original, notarized Builder Certification form.

### §119 Fee: TCO Payment ($100)
- **Source:** AC §26-214
- **Applicability:** NB or Alt 1 seeking a temporary CO.
- **How to Satisfy:** Pay fee at Cashier. Submit receipt to CO Unit.

### §120 Temp Construction Signoff
- **Source:** BC §27-214 – §27-218, AC §26-222, OPPN 6/93, 16/92, 5/92
- **Applicability:** NB or Alt 1 seeking a temporary CO.
- **How to Satisfy:** Obtain temporary Construction signoff for floors/spaces to be occupied.

### §124 Update Final Construction Signoff
- **Source:** BC §27-210
- **Applicability:** NB or Alt 1 if Final Construction Signoff was satisfied more than 1 year prior (or 18 months for residential).
- **How to Satisfy:** Obtain an updated final construction signoff from the Construction Division.
